About

Dr. Orhan Ermiş is a senior research associate in the CYBER Group / READY Unit at LIST. His academic and professional career spans cryptography, privacy-preserving technologies, network security, and machine learning, with extensive experience in both academic and industrial R&D projects across Europe.

Research Interests

  • Applied Cryptography
  • Privacy Enhancing Technologies
  • Machine Learning and Protection of Machine Learning Models
  • Computer and Network Security
  • Applications of Anomaly Detection
  • Computational Trust
